The Road to Glory

Editorial Infographic

Context

Infographic developed for a special publication about Argentina’s World Cup victory in Qatar 2022. The goal was to summarize the team’s full journey, from the qualifiers to the final, combining visual storytelling with statistical data.

Problem

The tournament coverage generated a large amount of scattered information: matches, results, statistics, and key moments. The challenge was to synthesize all this content into a single, clear, engaging, and easy-to-follow piece.

Solution

A visual narrative was designed based on a continuous, organic-shaped timeline that guides the reader from the qualifiers to the World Cup final. The journey is divided into two distinct stages: qualifiers and final stage, using color and visual direction to structure the reading flow.

Design Contribution

The piece integrates multiple layers of information into a single visual system: match-by-match results, key tournament milestones, and final statistics. The use of a central iconic shape reinforces the concept of journey and culmination, while color separates competitive contexts.

Result

The infographic successfully condenses the entire tournament into a clear and memorable narrative, enabling quick reading without losing informational depth. It works both as an editorial piece and as a visual summary of the championship.
